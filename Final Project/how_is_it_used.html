How is Geolocation used?

Geolocation is often used in social media apps. Many social media apps use geolocation data to let users to share their location with others, check in at places, and find nearby events. Snapchat's Snap Map is an example of geolocation. It let users know where their friends are located by using geolocation data.

Geolocation is also used in navigation and location based services. They use it for finding directions, discovering nearby restaurants, and finding a ride-sharing service. Waze is an example of navigation and location based services that uses geolocation. Waze can find your location and offers you directions to where you need to go.

Geolocation is also used in emergency service. It helps police, fire, and ambulance services, to locate people who need assistance and provide help. An example of emergency services using geolocation is ambulance. Ambulances use geolocation data to find their caller and provide help.
